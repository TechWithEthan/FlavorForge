About Us

Welcome to Flavor Forge Restaurant, where we're dedicated to serving the "working man" with delicious, no-frills food that's sure to satisfy. Our owner, Dave Jewett, has a passion for creating a dining experience that's both exceptional and approachable.

Located in the heart of Wichita, Kansas, our restaurant is situated in the former District Taqueria space, which we've transformed into a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our layout may be familiar, but our menu is anything but ordinary.

At Flavor Forge, we're committed to using only the freshest ingredients to craft dishes that are both flavorful and familiar. From our signature smash burgers to our mouth-watering bone-in pork chops, every bite is a testament to our dedication to quality and taste.

Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service, ensuring that every guest feels welcome and cared for. Whether you're stopping by for lunch or dinner, we can't wait to serve you and become your new favorite dining destination.
